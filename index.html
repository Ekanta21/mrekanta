About Me

Hi there! I’m an enthusiastic designer who loves to play with designs. I strive to create simple, original and impactful user experiences. I take on projects of all sizes and spend most of my work day building UI/UX & Graphic designs. I primarily work in Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, XD, Figma, After Effects and Affinity Editor for Designing purpose. I think in design just as much as code, and I find a way to keep a balance between design and development.

I now work remotely as a freelancer UI/UX & Graphic designer. I've designed and built many graphics, UI/UX & prototypes alone from start to finish. But it's only so much fun to be a one-man band. There's nothing better than riffing ideas around a team you love to work with and sharing the honor of a job well done.
